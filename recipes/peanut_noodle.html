Peanut Noodles

This recipe describes how to make a quick peanut noodle dish.

Ingredients

Peanut Sauce

  • Peanut Butter
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice Wine Vinegar
  • Sesane Oil
  • Honey
  • Ginger
  • Chill pepper flakes

Noodles and Toppings

  • Noodles
  • Peanuts and sesame seeds
  • Spring Onions

Method

  1. Cook the noodles, keep some noodle water and set aside.
  2. Make the peanut sauce by combining the peanut butter, soy sauce, sriracha, rice vinegar, sesame oil, honey, chilli pepper flakes, ginger and some noodle water. Whisk until smooth.
