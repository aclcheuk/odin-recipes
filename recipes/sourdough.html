Sourdough Recipe

This sourdough recipe is from theperfectloaf.com. The dough has a high hydration of around 80%.

Ingredients

Levain

  • 30 g Strong white bread flour
  • 30 g Whole wheat flour
  • 60 g Water
  • 30 g Sourdough Starter

Main Dough

  • 822 g Strong white bread flour
  • 64 g Whole wheat flour
  • 745 g Water
  • 151 g Ripe levain
  • 17 g Salt

Method

  1. 0 hrs: Levain - in a small container, mix the levain ingredients and keep at 25 °C for 5 hrs
  2. +3 hrs: Autolyse - Add 822 g Strong white bread flour, 64 g whole wheat flour, 650 g water and mix. Cover the bowl and rest for 2 hrs
  3. +5 hrs: Mix - Add 95 g water, 17 g salt and the ripe levain. Fold for 3 mins until the dough smooths.
  4. +5.25 hrs: Bulk Fermentation - 6x sets of stretch and folds; first three at 15 mins intervals, last three at 30 mins intervals
  5. +9.25 hrs: Divide & PreShape - Lightly flour work surface and scrape out dough. Divide dough in half. Shape each half into round shape. Let rest for 30 mins uncovered.
  6. +9.75 hrs: Shape - Shape the dough into a round or oval shape and place in proofing baskets. Leave overnight in fridge.
  7. +24 hrs: Bake - 230 °C for 20 mins in Dutch oven, remove lid and bake for a further 30 mins.
